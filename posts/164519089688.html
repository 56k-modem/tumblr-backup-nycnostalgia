Fear City

[T]he Council for Public Safety (police, firefighters and other unions) had plenty of material to publish a scaremongering pamphlet called “Welcome to Fear City: A Survival Guide for Visitors to the City of New York.”

If you had plans to visit NYC, you would probably swiftly abandon them after seeing this thing, which featured a skull on the cover. Just like the skull under your pretty face that would surely be slashed should you dare walk the streets after 6 p.m., or leave Midtown, or take the subway ever…

But few even saw it, as it received such negative publicity that it never even got distributed. (link)
