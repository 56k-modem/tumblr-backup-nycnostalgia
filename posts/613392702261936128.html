Empire State Building

Once again I’m atop a building on 22nd Street in Manhattan, looking uptown. We see, from left to right: the RCA Building, the Empire State Building, the Pan AM Building (by then MetLife), along with the Chrysler Building.

One image, scanned from a medium-format negative, by Richard Koenig; taken in the summer of 1995.
